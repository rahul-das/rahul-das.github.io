Our Story


The local Indian retail apparel market holds a massive potential of growth and delivering world class cloth quality to the Indian households. The market is semi-organised to some extent in certain parts of the country, also unorganised in some parts as traditionally people visit the stores on walk-in basis. Healthy signs can be seen as many stores/retailers have registered themselves as sellers. But what about online promotions of the stores or management of store operations or how to compete with the big guys?

We say disruption is required. And this is where WeeBuy steps in. We are a bunch of young people based out of “The City of Joy – Kolkata” looking to make an impactful difference in this space and deliver unique shopping experience to the customers.

Founded in March 2018, we are committed to shape up the local Indian retail apparel market digitally through innovation, honesty and conviction.
